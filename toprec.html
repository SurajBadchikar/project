Veg Maratha

  • Time: 25mins
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Oil 4 tbsp
Cumin seeds 1/2 tsp
Dried red chillies 2

Step 1:Heat up oil in a pan.

Step 2:Add cumin seeds, curry leaves, dried red chillies, and blended masala.

Step 3:Mix well and fry on medium heat for about 4-5 minutes.

Step 4:Add kitchen king masala, coriander powder, red chili powder, khoya, and salt.

Step 5:Mix well and fry on medium heat until it releases oil.

Step 6:Add water enough to make gravy.

Step 7:Mix well and cook the gravy on medium heat for about 4-5 minutes.

Step 8:Turn off the gas and veg Maratha is ready.

Step 9:For serving, in a bowl take the kofte and add gravy on it.

Step 10:You also can add gravy first in the bowl and place kofte in it.

Step 11:Veg Maratha goes well with naan, paratha, roti or jeera rice.
